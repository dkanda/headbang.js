headbang.js

Play a song based off the bpm of your headbang

How it works

Uses tracking.js (trackingjs.com) to monitor your face's y coordinate. Detect the bpm over the span of 5 seconds Use that bpm to find songs with that bpm with echonest api Use the song artist and title to play the song with youtube api
